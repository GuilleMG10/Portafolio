Cost Management System for Bolivian Entrepreneurs

⭐ Score 100/100 · Graduated with Honors Aug 2025 – Apr 2026
Overview

Full-stack system designed for Bolivian entrepreneurs to manage business costs, finances, and operations. My undergraduate thesis at UCB — scored 100/100 and graduated with honors. Built with microservices architecture designed for long-term scale.

Tech Stack
MicroservicesNestJSNext.jsPostgreSQLPrismaClean ArchitectureSOLIDRBACCloud DeployTypeScriptDocker
The Challenge

What problem needed solving

Bolivian entrepreneurs — especially small business owners — lack accessible tools to track their costs, revenue, and operational expenses. Most use informal methods (notebooks, WhatsApp) that make it impossible to analyze their financial health or make data-driven decisions.

The Solution

How I built it

Designed and built a full-stack system with a microservices backend (NestJS) and a React/Next.js frontend, deployed to cloud infrastructure. Applied Clean Architecture and SOLID principles throughout to ensure the system could scale and be maintained long-term. Included RBAC for multi-user business accounts.

Key Features
Microservices Architecture
Separated backend into independent services (auth, costs, reports, users) communicating via well-defined APIs — allowing independent scaling and deployment.
Complex Database Modeling
Relational database with complex entity relationships covering businesses, users, cost categories, transactions, and reporting periods.
Clean Architecture + SOLID
Applied Clean Architecture layers (domain, application, infrastructure) and all SOLID principles for a codebase designed to be maintained and extended.
Cloud Infrastructure
Full cloud deployment with environment configuration, CI/CD pipeline, and infrastructure designed for production reliability.
