PROJECT 02 · Construction · Management

Coninarde — Construction Management System

Live in production Feb 2026 – Present
Overview

Comprehensive management system used daily by construction company staff for project tracking, employee management, contract control, materials logistics, and budget monitoring across active construction sites.

Tech Stack
NestJSPostgreSQLTypeORMTypeScriptRBACJWTDockerCI/CD
The Challenge

What problem needed solving

Coninarde managed multiple active construction sites simultaneously with paper-based tracking — leading to errors in budget control, missed material orders, and no real-time visibility into project progress for management.

The Solution

How I built it

Designed a centralized REST API that unified all operational data: projects, employees, contracts, materials, and budgets. Role-based access control ensures each user type sees only what they need, and continuous deployment keeps the system always up to date.

Key Features
Project & Progress Tracking
Real-time tracking of construction progress across multiple active sites, with milestone management and status updates for management visibility.
Contract & Budget Management
Full lifecycle management for contracts with budget tracking, expense logging, and automatic alerts for budget thresholds.
Materials & Logistics
Inventory tracking for construction materials across sites, with order management and supplier coordination features.
Role-Based Access Control
Distinct access levels for management, site supervisors, and field staff — each seeing only the data relevant to their role.
Results
✓ Eliminated paper-based tracking across all active sites
✓ Real-time budget visibility for management
✓ Reduced material ordering errors through centralized inventory
✓ Daily active use by construction staff across multiple sites
