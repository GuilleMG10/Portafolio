PROJECT 03 · Fitness · SaaS

Gym Management Software

Live in production Jun 2023 – Present
Overview

Custom backend system for a gym managing memberships, attendance tracking, payment control, and user profiles. Deployed and actively used by gym staff and clients on a daily basis.

Tech Stack
Node.jsTypeScriptPostgreSQLJWTRBACPrisma
The Challenge

What problem needed solving

The gym was managing memberships manually with spreadsheets, leading to expired memberships going unnoticed, attendance records being inaccurate, and no way to track payment history per member.

The Solution

How I built it

Built a custom REST API tailored to the gym's exact operational workflow. The system handles the full member lifecycle: registration, membership assignment, attendance check-in, payment tracking, and role-based access for staff vs. members.

Key Features
Membership Management
Full membership lifecycle from registration to expiration, with automatic status tracking and renewal reminders.
Attendance Tracking
Daily attendance logging with check-in/check-out records and historical reports for staff analysis.
Payment Control
Payment history per member, overdue tracking, and billing management adapted to the gym's payment model.
Staff vs Member Access
Role-based system that gives staff full operational access while members can only view their own profile and attendance.
Results
✓ Eliminated manual spreadsheet tracking for 100+ members
✓ Staff can process check-ins in seconds instead of minutes
✓ Zero expired memberships going unnoticed since launch
✓ Daily active use by gym staff since Jun 2023
