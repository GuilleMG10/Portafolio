PROJECT 04 · Healthcare · Admin System

Estética Clínica — Admin System

Live in production Jun 2023 – Present
Overview

Administrative backend for an aesthetic clinic handling appointments, patient records, treatment history, and staff roles — designed around the clinic's daily operational workflow and built for reliability.

Tech Stack
NestJSPrismaPostgreSQLJWTRBACTypeScript
The Challenge

What problem needed solving

The clinic had no digital system for managing patient records or appointments — everything was done on paper or in WhatsApp groups. This caused double-bookings, lost patient histories, and no way to track treatment progress over time.

The Solution

How I built it

Built a secure backend system with patient records, appointment scheduling, treatment history, and staff management. All data is stored with proper relational modeling so any staff member can pull up a patient's complete history instantly.

Key Features
Appointment Management
Full scheduling system with conflict prevention, status tracking (confirmed, cancelled, completed), and daily agenda views for staff.
Patient Records
Complete patient profiles with contact info, medical notes, allergies, and treatment preferences — accessible to authorized staff instantly.
Treatment History
Chronological treatment log per patient with procedure details, products used, and staff notes for continuity of care.
Staff Roles & Access
Distinct permissions for receptionists (appointments only), practitioners (full patient access), and admin (system configuration).
Results
✓ Eliminated double-bookings completely since launch
✓ Full patient history accessible in seconds instead of searching paper records
✓ Staff onboarding reduced — new staff can access patient history immediately
✓ Daily operational use since Jun 2023
